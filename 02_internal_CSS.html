Svi Poly Latur Admission Process

Admission in Svi Poly, Latur gets done through Centralized Admission Process (CAP). The process, being quite simple, involves the following steps:

  • Eligibility: The candidate should have passed the 10th standard for regular diploma courses or 10 +2 or MCVC ITI for lateral entry courses from any recognized board.
  • Application Form: Collect the application form, fill the form, upload all documents, and pay the application fee.
  • Merit List and Seat Allotment: The merit list is prepared on the basis of marks obtained in SSC (class 10) and qualifying open examination.
  • Appear for Counselling: If eligible, candidates will be called for counselling while they have to Register for CAP.
  • Institute-level Verification: Carry the following documents:
  • Fee receipts High school marksheets and intermediate mark sheets
  • Reserved category certificate, if applicable
  • Income and residence certificates
  • Character, transfer, and medical certificates
  • Gap affidavit, if applicable
  • 4 passport size photographs
  • Anti-ragging affidavit
