Welcome to Swami Vivekanand institute of polytechnic,latur

Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic,latur was established in the year 2013 at Latur, Maharashtra. The institute was conceived under a Central Sector Scheme for imparting quality technical education in engineering disciplines. The institution is situated at JSPM Campus,, Barshi Road, District Latur, Maharashtra 413531,

Regular Polytechnic Courses (3 Years):

  1. Computer Engineering
  2. Information Technology
  3. Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
  4. Civil Engineering
  5. Mechanical Engineering
  6. Electrical Engineering
  7. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML)
